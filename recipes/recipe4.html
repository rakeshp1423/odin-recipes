Description

This Garlic Butter Shrimp is a simple and delicious dish that is perfect for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  2. Step 2

    Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.

  3. Step 3

    Stir in the chicken broth and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.

Cook's Note :

This recipe can be served over pasta, rice, or with crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.
